Discover the journey of the Internet through decades of innovation.

Internet Timeline

1960s – ARPANET

The U.S. Department of Defense created ARPANET, the first network to implement packet switching, laying the foundation of the Internet.

1980s – Expansion

Universities and research institutions connected, email became popular, and TCP/IP protocol was adopted as the standard.

1990s – The World Wide Web

Tim Berners-Lee introduced the World Wide Web, making the Internet accessible to the public with websites and browsers.

2000s – Broadband & Social Media

High-speed Internet spread globally. Platforms like Facebook, YouTube, and Twitter transformed online communication.

Present – Cloud & Mobile Internet

The Internet is now central to daily life, powering cloud computing, mobile apps, online learning, and global communication.
